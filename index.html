The system

A practical, living reference — not a static PDF. The color engine is interactive: pick a Tailwind preset or paste a brand hex and it builds the full accessible ramp. Fine-tune hue & chroma for print-sourced colors, then lock the palette — it persists for next time.

Color

Brand colors — up to 4 primary and 4 secondary — each generate a full 50–950 ramp on Tailwind's L/C curve. System colors are the fixed Tailwind v4 families; expand to browse all 22.
